# Frameworks Reference

## 2x2 Matrix: Common Axis Pairs for Content Account Positioning

*Depth vs. Frequency:* Content depth (technical/detailed vs. accessible/brief) × Posting frequency (daily vs. weekly)

*Niche vs. Reach:* Topic specificity (narrow niche vs. broad appeal) × Audience size (large vs. small)

*Original Research vs. Curation:* Content originality (primary research vs. aggregation/commentary) × Production quality (high vs. low)

*Technical vs. Accessible:* Complexity level (expert-level vs. beginner-friendly) × Entertainment value (educational vs. entertaining)

*Platform vs. Cross-platform:* Platform focus (single-platform vs. multi-platform) × Audience engagement depth (surface vs. community)

*Short-form vs. Long-form:* Content length (short clips/posts vs. long video/articles) × Monetization potential (ad revenue vs. direct sales)

## Tier Diagram

Use when accounts cluster naturally by audience size:

```
Tier 1 (Mega): 1M+ followers — [Account A], [Account B]
Tier 2 (Macro): 100K–1M — [Account C], [Account D]
Tier 3 (Mid): 10K–100K — [Account E], [Account F]
Tier 4 (Micro): <10K — [Account G]
```

## Radar Dimensions for AI Content Accounts

When comparing across 5-6 factors, use:
- Technical depth (1-5)
- Production quality (1-5)
- Posting consistency (1-5)
- Audience engagement (1-5)
- Topic diversity (1-5)
- Cross-platform presence (1-5)
